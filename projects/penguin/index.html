Packed With Fun

Everything a kid needs to practice math and typing while having a blast

🧩

Multiplication Mode

Pick your times tables (2s through 10s or Mixed) and type your answers. Perfect for building math fluency!

🎤

Voice Math Mode

Say your answers out loud! Uses Apple's speech recognition so kids who can't type yet can still play.

⌨️

Typing Practice

Practice home row, top row, bottom row, or all keys. Letters appear and you race to press them!

🏁

Race Against AI

Compete against 3 penguin opponents — Frosty, Waddles, and Chilly. Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty.

📊

Track Your Progress

See your time, correct answers, missed answers, best streak, and letters per minute after every race.

🐧

Customize Your Penguin

Pick from 6 colors, choose a fun name, and make the penguin yours. 25 fun penguin facts to discover!

How To Play

Jump from ice chunk to ice chunk by answering correctly!

1

Pick Your Mode

Choose Multiplication, Voice Math, or Typing from the main menu

2

Customize

Pick your penguin color, type your name, and choose your challenge level

3

Race!

Answer questions to jump across ice. Wrong answers splash you in the water!

4

Win!

Beat the AI penguins to the finish line and celebrate with confetti!
